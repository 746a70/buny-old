buny is a DJ, producer, event organizer, and educator based out of vancouver, canada. they're known for spinning high energy, experimental sets that typically fuse elements of techno, bass, house, and deconstructed club. from flagship festivals like FVDED in the Park to underground raves organized by the likes of Normiecorp, B_Drive, and G_H_O_S_T, buny's sets captivate attention and push the boundaries of club music through a uniquely queer lens.


they are the host of "bunys world", a monthly radio show hosted on bside radio. they also work as an event organizer, known for pioneering Rewind Resale's infamous thrift store raves [2022-2024] and organizing community-focused parties focused on supporting trans, BIPOC, and marginalized artists. most recently, they debuted "THUMP" in april 2025, their new independent queer party series.


as an educator, they established "hop in" in january 2024; a free monthly workshop dedicated to teaching beginner DJs the basics of CDJs. in collaboration with DJ and event organizer TR4CK ID?, they were the recipient of Canada's Youth Small Grants and expanded their workshops to "hop in: DJ intensive", a femme and BIPOC focused multi-week intensive aimed to uplift DJs by educating on technical gear knowledge, business practices, and marketing skills.
